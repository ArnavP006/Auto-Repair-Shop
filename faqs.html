Common Questions

How often should I get my oil changed? +
Most manufacturers recommend changing your oil every 5,000 to 7,500 miles for conventional oil, or every 10,000 to 15,000 miles for synthetic oil. However, this can vary based on your driving habits and vehicle type. Check your owner's manual for specific recommendations, and our technicians can help you determine the best schedule for your vehicle.
Do you offer warranty on your repairs? +
Yes! We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. Most repairs come with a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty, whichever comes first. Specific warranty terms may vary depending on the type of repair and parts used. We'll always discuss warranty coverage before beginning any work.
How long does a typical service take? +
Service times vary depending on the type of work needed. A basic oil change typically takes 30-45 minutes, while more complex repairs like brake replacement or transmission work may take several hours or even a full day. We'll provide you with an estimated completion time when you schedule your appointment.
Do I need an appointment or can I walk in? +
While we do accept walk-ins, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can accommodate you promptly. Appointments help us manage our workflow and minimize your wait time. You can book online through our Select page or call us directly.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, cash, and digital payment methods like UPI, Google Pay, and PhonePe. We also offer financing options for larger repairs through our partner financial institutions.
Do you use genuine OEM parts? +
Yes, we primarily use genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts to ensure the best quality and compatibility with your vehicle. In some cases, we may offer high-quality aftermarket alternatives that meet or exceed OEM specifications, which can provide cost savings without compromising quality.
Can you work on all makes and models? +
Absolutely! Our certified technicians are trained to work on all makes and models, from domestic to foreign, economy to luxury vehicles. We have the diagnostic equipment and expertise to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs on any vehicle.
Do you offer pick-up and drop-off service? +
Yes! We offer complimentary pick-up and drop-off service within a 10-mile radius of our shop for major repairs. This service must be scheduled in advance and is subject to availability. Contact us to arrange this convenient service.
How do I know if my brakes need replacing? +
Common signs include squealing or grinding noises, vibration when braking, longer stopping distances, or a brake warning light on your dashboard. If you notice any of these symptoms, bring your vehicle in for a free brake inspection. We'll check your brake pads, rotors, and fluid to determine what's needed.
What should I do if my check engine light comes on? +
Don't panic! While a check engine light can indicate various issues, many are minor. Bring your vehicle to us for a diagnostic scan. We'll read the error codes, identify the problem, and provide you with a detailed explanation and repair estimate. If the light is flashing, this indicates a more serious issue - contact us immediately or have your vehicle towed.
Are your technicians certified? +
Yes! All our technicians are ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified and undergo continuous training to stay updated with the latest automotive technologies. We also have manufacturer-specific certifications for major brands like Honda, Toyota, BMW, and Mercedes.
How much does a typical brake job cost? +
Brake service costs vary depending on what's needed. A basic brake pad replacement typically ranges from ₹3,500 to ₹8,000 per axle. If rotors need replacement or machining, costs may increase. We always provide a detailed estimate before beginning any work, and we'll never perform unnecessary services.
Do you provide loaner cars or shuttle service? +
Yes! We offer complimentary shuttle service within a 10-mile radius for your convenience. For major repairs that require your vehicle to stay overnight, we can arrange a loaner vehicle based on availability. Please request this when scheduling your appointment.
Can I wait while my car is being serviced? +
Absolutely! We have a comfortable waiting area with complimentary Wi-Fi, refreshments, and entertainment. For quick services like oil changes, most customers prefer to wait. For longer repairs, we recommend using our shuttle service or scheduling a drop-off appointment.
What if I need emergency roadside assistance? +
We offer 24/7 emergency roadside assistance for our customers. If you're stranded, call our emergency hotline at +91 98765 43210. We provide towing services, jump starts, tire changes, and fuel delivery. Our team will get you back on the road or safely to our shop.
How often should I service my car? +
We recommend following your vehicle manufacturer's maintenance schedule, typically every 5,000-10,000 km or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance includes oil changes, tire rotations, brake inspections, and fluid checks. We'll create a personalized maintenance schedule based on your vehicle and driving habits.
Do you work on hybrid and electric vehicles? +
Yes! Our technicians are specially trained and certified to work on hybrid and electric vehicles. We have the specialized equipment and knowledge to safely service high-voltage systems, battery packs, and electric motors. We service all major hybrid and EV brands.
What's included in your multi-point inspection? +
Our complimentary multi-point inspection includes: checking all fluid levels, inspecting brakes and brake pads, examining tires for wear and proper inflation, testing battery health, inspecting belts and hoses, checking lights and wipers, examining suspension components, and scanning for any visible leaks or damage. You'll receive a detailed report.
Can you help with insurance claims for accident repairs? +
Yes! We work with all major insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process. We'll provide detailed estimates, photos, and documentation needed for your claim. Our team can communicate directly with your insurance adjuster to ensure a smooth repair process.
Do you offer any discounts or special offers? +
Yes! We regularly offer seasonal promotions and service specials. We also provide discounts for senior citizens, military personnel, and students. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay informed about current offers. Ask about our loyalty program for returning customers.
