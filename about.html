About AutoMechanica

Excellence in Auto Repair Since 1999

Our Story

AutoMechanica was founded in 1999 with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and professional auto repair services to our community. Over the past 25 years, we've grown from a small garage to a full-service automotive repair center, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has never wavered.

Our team of certified technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and genuine OEM parts to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.

We believe in transparency, which is why we always provide detailed explanations of needed repairs and upfront pricing. Your trust is our most valuable asset, and we work hard every day to earn and maintain it.
